Dominican Cuisine

The Dominican Republic offers a rich culinary heritage influenced by Spanish, African, and indigenous ingredients. Popular dishes are:

  • Mangu - Mashed plantains served with fried cheese and eggs.
  • Sancocho - A stew made with meat, vegetables, and root crops.
  • Mofongo - Fried plantain mashed with garlic, pork, and other ingredients.
