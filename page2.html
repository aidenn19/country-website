The national animal of the Dominican Republic is the palmchat, a small, long-tailed bird that is native to the Caribbean island of Hispaniola:

  • Palmchat
  • Size About 8 inches in size
  • Color Dark brown above with an olive green wash on the wing
  • Behavior Associated with palms for feeding, roosting, and nesting
